Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ComplianceLogger

How does ComplianceLogger help if I've already lost waste disposal records?

ComplianceLogger includes a record reconstruction tool that uses invoices, photos, and hauler testimony to rebuild missing documentation with transparent confidence tracking. Legally defensible for audits when disclosed honestly.

Is $149/year really enough for EPA compliance software?

Yes. ComplianceLogger is designed for small/mid-size contractors who need compliance without enterprise pricing. One avoided RCRA violation (penalties can reach tens of thousands of dollars) pays for decades of software.

What if I get audited? Can ComplianceLogger generate reports for the EPA?

Yes. One-click export of all waste records in EPA-compliant format. Includes manifests, invoices, photos, and disposal site confirmations organized by project and date.

Do I need to be a tech expert to use this?

No. ComplianceLogger is designed for contractors, not IT staff. Log waste in 30 seconds: take photo of dumpster, enter quantity, done. Simple mobile interface.

Can I track waste for multiple job sites at once?

Yes. Unlimited projects. Each project gets its own waste log. Filter by date, site, waste type, or hauler.

What waste types does ComplianceLogger track?

Construction debris, hazardous waste (asbestos, lead, contaminated soil), universal waste (batteries, electronics), and special waste (medical, chemical). Covers RCRA and state regulations.

Will ComplianceLogger work if my crew isn't good with technology?

Yes. Mobile app has big buttons, photo-first interface, and minimal typing. If your crew can take a phone photo, they can log waste.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

You keep it. Export all records as PDF or CSV before canceling. Your data is yours forever. We don't hold it hostage.

Does ComplianceLogger guarantee I won't get fined?

No software can guarantee zero fines (that depends on your actual practices), but ComplianceLogger ensures you have the documentation the EPA requires. Good records dramatically reduce penalties.

Can I try it before committing to $149/year?

Yes. 7-day free trial with full features. Test it on your current projects. No credit card required until you decide to subscribe.

What states does this work in? I'm in [State].

All 50 states. ComplianceLogger covers federal EPA requirements (RCRA, CERCLA) plus state-specific regulations. Regulations database updated monthly.

How long does ComplianceLogger keep records?

Minimum 3 years (federal RCRA requirement per 40 CFR 262.40), though some states require longer. You can keep records as long as needed. Export and archive anytime.

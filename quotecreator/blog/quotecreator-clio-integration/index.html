QuoteCreator + Clio Integration: Seamless Estimates for Legal Professionals

📅 October 12, 2025 📖 8 min read

For legal professionals, time is literally money. Every minute spent on administrative tasks—creating estimates, sending quotes to clients, uploading documents to case files—is time not spent on billable work. That's where QuoteCreator's Clio integration comes in.

QuoteCreator is a browser-based estimate and quote app designed for professionals who need to create accurate, professional quotes quickly. When integrated with Clio (the leading legal practice management software), it becomes a powerful tool for streamlining your billing workflow.

Why Legal Professionals Need Fast Estimates

Legal work often requires quick turnaround on estimates and quotes. Whether you're:

  • Meeting with a potential client who wants to know upfront costs for representation
  • Providing a retainer estimate for a new case
  • Quoting project-based legal services (document review, contract drafting, etc.)
  • Estimating additional costs mid-case for scope changes

You need a solution that's fast, professional, and integrates seamlessly with your existing practice management tools.

💡 Pro Tip: Mobile estimates mean you can create and send quotes during client meetings, consultations, or even court breaks—no need to return to the office and remember details later.

The Clio Integration Advantage

QuoteCreator's Clio integration brings together mobile estimate creation and automatic document management. Here's how it works:

1. OAuth 2.0 Secure Authentication

QuoteCreator connects to Clio using OAuth 2.0, the industry-standard security protocol. This means:

  • No password storage—QuoteCreator never sees or stores your Clio credentials
  • Revocable access—You can disconnect QuoteCreator from Clio anytime via Clio's settings
  • Permission-based access—QuoteCreator only requests the minimum permissions needed (matter read access, document upload)

2. Matter Sync and Search

Once connected, QuoteCreator can:

  • Browse your Clio matters—All client matters appear in QuoteCreator's interface
  • Search by client name or matter number—Find the right case instantly
  • Associate estimates with specific matters—Link each quote to the correct client file

3. One-Tap Upload to Client Files

After creating an estimate in QuoteCreator, simply tap "Upload to Clio" to:

  • Automatically save the PDF to the correct matter—No manual file uploads or downloads
  • Preserve professional formatting—PDF estimates are uploaded exactly as generated
  • Maintain audit trail—Clio timestamps all uploaded documents for compliance

Step-by-Step Setup Guide

Setting up QuoteCreator's Clio integration takes less than 2 minutes. Here's the complete walkthrough:

Step 1: Open QuoteCreator

Access QuoteCreator at quotecreator-web-kr6aj.ondigitalocean.app. The app works in any modern browser on desktop or mobile.

Step 2: Connect to Clio

From QuoteCreator's settings:

  1. Tap Integrations → Connect Clio
  2. You'll be redirected to Clio's OAuth login page
  3. Sign in with your Clio credentials (or use SSO if enabled)
  4. Review permissions and tap Authorize
  5. QuoteCreator will confirm successful connection

For detailed instructions with screenshots, see our Complete Clio Setup Guide.

Step 3: Create Your First Estimate

With Clio connected, creating estimates becomes seamless:

  1. Open QuoteCreator and tap New Estimate
  2. Add customer details (or select from your saved contacts)
  3. Select Clio Matter from the dropdown (QuoteCreator will show all your matters)
  4. Add line items (hourly rates, flat fees, or project estimates)
  5. Tap Generate PDF to create the professional estimate
  6. Tap Upload to Clio to save directly to the matter file

That's it! The entire process takes under 60 seconds once you're familiar with the workflow.

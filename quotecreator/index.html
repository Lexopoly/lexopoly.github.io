Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about QuoteCreator

Is QuoteCreator really a one-time purchase with no subscription?

Yes. Pay $12.99 once, use forever. No monthly fees, no per-quote charges, no hidden costs.

Can I use QuoteCreator on my phone while at a job site?

Yes. QuoteCreator works in any modern web browser on desktop, tablet, and mobile. Create estimates on-site, email them to clients immediately from your phone.

Does it work offline? I often lose signal at construction sites.

Yes. QuoteCreator works 100% offline. Create, save, and edit estimates without internet. Email them when you're back in range.

Can I customize the estimate template with my company logo?

Yes. Add your logo, company info, terms & conditions, payment instructions, and custom branding colors.

Does QuoteCreator integrate with Clio for lawyers?

Yes. Create estimates in QuoteCreator, sync them to Clio client files automatically. Saves 10-15 minutes per estimate for legal professionals.

What if I need to make changes to an estimate after sending it?

Edit anytime. QuoteCreator saves all estimates locally. Revise and resend updated versions - no limit on revisions.

Can I track which estimates were accepted vs rejected?

Yes. Mark estimates as "Pending", "Accepted", or "Declined". View conversion rates and follow up on pending quotes.

Is there a limit on how many estimates I can create?

No. Create unlimited estimates. The $12.99 one-time purchase includes unlimited use forever.

What formats can I export estimates to?

PDF (most popular), email directly from app, or print. PDFs work on any device and look professional.

Do you offer a money-back guarantee?

Yes. 30-day full refund if you're not satisfied. No questions asked.

QuoteCreator Data Deletion

Page Purpose: Instructions for deleting your QuoteCreator data from your Android device
Last Updated: October 10, 2025

Local-First App = Easy Data Deletion: QuoteCreator stores ALL your data locally on your Android device. To delete your data, simply uninstall the app or clear app data through Android settings. No server-side data exists to delete.

What Data Does QuoteCreator Store?

QuoteCreator is a local-first application that stores all data exclusively on your Android device in a SQLite database:

  • Estimates and quotes you've created
  • Line items and pricing information
  • Customer information (if you've entered any)
  • Company settings and branding preferences
  • Clio OAuth tokens (encrypted, if you've connected to Clio)

Important: We do NOT store any of your business data on our servers. Everything stays on your device.

How to Delete Your Data

Choose one of the following methods to permanently delete all QuoteCreator data from your Android device:

Method 1: Uninstall the App (Recommended)

  1. Open your Android device's Settings
  2. Navigate to Apps (or Applications)
  3. Find and tap QuoteCreator
  4. Tap Uninstall
  5. Confirm the uninstallation

Result: All QuoteCreator data is permanently deleted from your device, including estimates, settings, and Clio OAuth tokens.

Method 2: Clear App Data (Keep the App Installed)

  1. Open your Android device's Settings
  2. Navigate to Apps (or Applications)
  3. Find and tap QuoteCreator
  4. Tap Storage (or Storage & cache)
  5. Tap Clear Data (or Clear Storage)
  6. Confirm the action

Result: All QuoteCreator data is permanently deleted, but the app remains installed. You can start fresh as if you just downloaded it.

⚠️ Warning: Both methods permanently delete all your estimates, quotes, customer information, and settings. This action cannot be undone. We recommend exporting important estimates as PDFs before deleting data.

What About PDFs and Clio Data?

PDF Files

If you've generated and saved PDF files to your device's Downloads folder, these files are stored separately from the app:

  • Uninstalling or clearing app data does NOT delete PDF files
  • To delete PDFs, open your Files app and manually delete them from the Downloads folder

Clio Uploaded Documents

If you've used the "Upload to Clio" feature, those documents now exist in your Clio account:

  • Deleting QuoteCreator data does NOT delete documents already uploaded to Clio
  • To delete documents from Clio, log in to Clio and delete them from the relevant matter
  • To disconnect QuoteCreator from your Clio account, go to QuoteCreator Settings → Disconnect Clio (before uninstalling)

Revoking Clio Access

If you want to revoke QuoteCreator's access to your Clio account:

  1. Log in to Clio
  2. Go to Settings → Integrations → Connected Apps
  3. Find QuoteCreator and click Revoke Access

This permanently prevents QuoteCreator from accessing your Clio account, even if you reinstall the app.

Verification

To verify that your data has been deleted:

  • If you uninstalled: QuoteCreator will no longer appear in your app list
  • If you cleared data: Open QuoteCreator and you'll see the initial setup screen (as if it's a fresh install)

Server-side verification not needed: Since we don't store your business data on any servers, there's no server-side data to check or delete.

What We Delete (From Our Side)

Lexopoly does not store QuoteCreator user data on our servers, so there's nothing for us to delete. However:

  • Google Play purchase records: Managed by Google (not us). See Google's data deletion policy
  • Support emails: If you've contacted support@lexopoly.com, we may have records of your support requests. Email us to request deletion of support correspondence

Data Deletion Timeframe

  • Device data: Deleted immediately upon uninstall or clearing app data
  • Support correspondence: Deleted within 30 days of your email request to support@lexopoly.com
  • Clio OAuth tokens: Immediately invalidated when you disconnect or revoke access

Your Rights

Under privacy laws like GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), and PIPEDA (Canada), you have the right to:

  • Access your data: All your data is in your device's app storage (Settings → Apps → QuoteCreator → Storage)
  • Delete your data: Use the methods described above
  • Export your data: Generate PDFs of your estimates and save them to any location
  • Revoke third-party access: Disconnect Clio integration at any time

Need Help?

If you have questions about data deletion or need assistance:

  • Email: support@lexopoly.com
  • Subject line: "QuoteCreator Data Deletion Request"
  • Response time: Within 2 business days

We're happy to help you delete any server-side data we may have (like support emails) or answer questions about the deletion process.

Summary: QuoteCreator is a local-first app. All your business data is stored on your device. To delete it, simply uninstall the app or clear app data through Android settings. No server-side data exists to delete.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about LocalTranscribe

Is LocalTranscribe really 100% offline?

Yes. All processing happens on your device. No audio ever leaves your computer. You can disconnect from the internet and still transcribe.

What audio file formats does LocalTranscribe support?

MP3, WAV, M4A, FLAC, OGG, and most other common formats. Files up to 4 hours long.

How accurate is the transcription compared to cloud services?

LocalTranscribe uses the same AI models as leading cloud services (Whisper by OpenAI), so accuracy is identical - typically 95-98% for clear audio. The difference is privacy, not quality.

Can I use LocalTranscribe for attorney-client privileged communications?

Yes. Many law firms use LocalTranscribe specifically because it doesn't upload audio to third parties, preserving attorney-client privilege. Cloud transcription services may waive privilege.

What are the system requirements?

Windows 10/11, macOS 10.15+, or Linux. 8GB RAM minimum (16GB recommended). 2GB free disk space. No GPU required.

Is there a volume limit or per-minute charge?

No. Unlimited transcription for your $39-79/user monthly plan. Transcribe 1 hour or 1,000 hours - same price.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes. 7-day free trial with full features. No credit card required. Cancel anytime.

What if I need to transcribe in a language other than English?

LocalTranscribe supports 90+ languages including Spanish, French, German, Mandarin, Japanese, and more. Same accuracy, same offline processing.

Can multiple users share transcriptions in our office?

Each user needs their own license, but you can export transcripts as text files to share. Volume discounts available for 10+ users.

What happens if I need help or technical support?

Email support typically responds within 4 business hours. Live chat available for urgent issues during business hours (9am-5pm EST).

What to Expect After Contacting Us

1. Initial Response (1-2 business days)

We'll review your pilot request and respond with next steps. If you're a good fit for our pilot cohort, we'll schedule a brief introductory call.

2. Introductory Call (15-20 minutes)

We'll discuss your current billing challenges, explain how RevenueRescue works, and answer any technical questions. This is also where we'll share pilot program details and pricing.

3. Onboarding (2-5 minutes)

If you decide to join the pilot, we'll send you access credentials. Setup is simple: connect your Clio account via OAuth (click a button), approve calendar access, done. First suggestions appear within 24 hours.

4. Ongoing Support

Pilot participants receive priority email support. We'll check in weekly during your first month to ensure you're getting maximum value from RevenueRescue.

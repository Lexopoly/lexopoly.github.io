Frequently Asked Questions

How does RevenueRescue detect unbilled time?

RevenueRescue connects to your Clio calendar using secure authentication and monitors your calendar events in real-time. It identifies client-related activities (meetings, calls, consultations) and filters out internal/non-billable events. The AI analyzes event titles, participants, and duration to generate appropriate billing suggestions.

Is my Clio data secure?

Yes, absolutely. We use secure authentication, which means we never see or store your Clio password. Your firm's data is kept completely separate from other firms.

What's the ROI for my firm?

Research shows law firms lose 10-20% of billable time to forgotten activities. For a solo practitioner billing $350/hour, RevenueRescue typically recovers $14K-$36K annually. After the $1,800 annual cost, net benefit is $12,200-$34,200 (7-19× return). Payback period is typically 18-30 days. Visit our pricing page to calculate your specific ROI.

Do I need to change how I work?

No. RevenueRescue works invisibly in the background. You continue your day as normal—meeting with clients, making calls, sending emails. The system monitors your calendar automatically. At your convenience (typically end of day or week), you review suggestions and approve with one click. No workflow changes required.

When can I start using RevenueRescue?

We're currently in pilot phase with limited availability. Contact us for early access to join our pilot cohort. We're finalizing trial terms and onboarding process. Pilot participants will receive priority access and special pricing consideration.

What if I accidentally approve a non-billable suggestion?

RevenueRescue includes undo capability for recently approved entries. If you realize you approved something by mistake (like a personal appointment or internal meeting), simply click the undo button and that entry won't post to your Clio time sheet. You maintain complete control over every billable entry—mistakes are easy to fix.

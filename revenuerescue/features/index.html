Core Features

Six powerful capabilities working together to capture every billable minute

1. Calendar Monitoring

Continuous automatic detection of billable calendar events. RevenueRescue works invisibly in the background, monitoring your Clio calendar 24/7 without requiring any manual input.

  • Real-time monitoring of calendar events as they occur
  • OAuth 2.0 secure authentication - we never see your password
  • Intelligent filtering between client and internal events
  • Event participant analysis to identify client interactions
  • Duration tracking with automatic rounding to billing increments
  • Never forgets, never sleeps, always watching for billable activities
  • Works with recurring meetings and one-time appointments
  • Handles last-minute calendar changes and updates
📅

2. AI Billing Suggestions

Smart AI generates professional billing narratives automatically, following your firm's standards and formatting conventions. Each suggestion includes time calculations, matter codes, and activity descriptions.

  • Professional narrative generation following legal billing standards
  • Automatic time calculations (duration to billable increments)
  • Matter code detection from event titles and participants
  • Activity code assignment based on meeting type
  • Context-aware descriptions (calls vs meetings vs consultations)
  • Client name extraction and verification
  • Proper capitalization and formatting
  • Learns from your approval patterns to improve suggestions
🤖

3. One-Click Approval Workflow

Review and approve billing suggestions in seconds, not minutes. Reject non-billable activities, edit narratives before posting, or approve in bulk. The entire process is designed for speed and efficiency.

  • Single-click approval posts directly to Clio time entries
  • Bulk actions supported - approve multiple suggestions at once
  • Reject button for non-billable activities (admin time, internal meetings)
  • Edit narratives, times, or matter codes before approving
  • Approval history tracking for accountability
  • Undo capability for recently approved entries - Mistakes happen. If you accidentally approve a personal appointment or internal meeting, click undo to remove it before posting to Clio. Complete control with a safety net.
  • Keyboard shortcuts for power users (spacebar to approve, X to reject)
  • Mobile-friendly interface for on-the-go approvals
✓

4. Native Clio Integration

Seamless OAuth integration with Clio Manage means no API keys, no manual configuration, no data export/import. Approved suggestions post directly as time entries with proper matter codes, activity codes, and billing rates.

  • OAuth 2.0 authentication - no API keys or passwords to manage
  • Direct time entry creation in Clio (no CSV imports)
  • Automatic matter and activity code sync
  • Contact matching and verification
  • Billing rate application from Clio settings
  • Respects Clio's billable/non-billable flags
  • Supports Clio's custom activity codes
  • Real-time sync - changes reflected immediately
🔗

5. Revenue Dashboard & Analytics

Visualize captured revenue vs potential opportunities. Track improvement over time, identify patterns in unbilled activities, and measure the financial impact of RevenueRescue on your firm's bottom line.

  • Dashboard showing captured vs missed revenue
  • Weekly and monthly trend analysis
  • Pattern identification (which types of activities most often missed)
  • Attorney-level performance tracking (for firm administrators)
  • ROI calculation showing RevenueRescue's financial impact
  • Export reports for accounting and management review
  • Time-of-day analysis (when are most activities forgotten?)
  • Matter-level revenue recovery tracking
📊

6. Multi-Matter & Multi-Attorney Support

Handles complex scenarios where attorneys work on multiple active matters simultaneously. Smart detection algorithms match calendar events to the correct client matters without manual intervention.

  • Automatic matter detection from event titles and participants
  • Handles attorneys with 50+ active client matters
  • Matter switching without re-authentication
  • Firm-wide deployment supporting unlimited attorneys
  • Role-based access (attorneys see only their suggestions, admins see firm-wide)
  • Multi-client meeting support (split time across matters)
  • Conflict-of-interest awareness (flags potential conflicts)
  • Matter closing detection (suggests final billing review)
💼

  • Daily automatic refresh - new suggestions every morning
  • Continuous monitoring mode (real-time) or batch processing (daily)
  • One-time historical backfill when you first connect

  • Handles timezone differences for multi-office firms

Real-World Use Cases

Use Case 1: Solo Practitioner Revenue Recovery

Scenario: Sarah is a solo estate planning attorney billing $350/hour. She's busy with client meetings and often forgets to log calls and brief consultations. She estimates she loses about 1 hour per day to forgotten time entries - approximately $30,000 per year in lost revenue.

Solution with RevenueRescue:

  1. Sarah connects RevenueRescue to her Clio account (2 minutes setup)
  2. RevenueRescue analyzes her last 90 days of calendar history
  3. Immediately identifies 47 unbilled activities worth $8,200
  4. Going forward, Sarah receives daily suggestion emails each morning
  5. She spends 10 minutes reviewing and approving suggestions (vs 60 minutes manual entry)
  6. RevenueRescue captures 90% of previously forgotten activities

Outcome: Sarah recovers $27,000 in the first year (90% of lost revenue). After the $1,800 annual cost ($149/month), net benefit is $25,200 - a 14× return on investment. Plus she saves 208 hours per year on time entry (5 work weeks).

Use Case 2: Small Firm Standardization

Scenario: Thompson & Associates is a 5-attorney personal injury firm. Billing practices vary by attorney - some enter time daily, others weekly (or later). The firm suspects they're losing $100K+ annually to forgotten time, but lacks data. Managing partner wants standardized billing suggestions across all attorneys.

Solution with RevenueRescue:

  1. Firm administrator deploys RevenueRescue firm-wide (5 attorneys)
  2. Each attorney connects their Clio calendar independently
  3. RevenueRescue provides firm-wide analytics dashboard
  4. Managing partner identifies patterns: junior attorneys miss 25% of calls
  5. AI suggestions standardize narrative quality across all attorneys
  6. Firm captures additional $120,000 in billable time over 12 months

Outcome: $120,000 additional revenue across 5 attorneys. Annual cost: $8,940 ($149/month × 5 attorneys). Net benefit: $111,060 (12× ROI). Plus standardized billing quality improves client perception and reduces write-downs.

Use Case 3: Time Entry Burden Reduction

Scenario: Michael is a litigation partner billing $600/hour. He spends 60 minutes per day on manual time entry, reconstructing his activities from memory and notes. That's 250 hours per year on administrative work worth $150,000 in potential billable time. He's frustrated by the time entry burden and accuracy concerns.

Solution with RevenueRescue:

  1. Michael connects RevenueRescue to offload time entry burden
  2. Instead of 60 min/day reconstructing from memory, spends 10 min/day reviewing suggestions
  3. RevenueRescue captures calendar activities automatically
  4. Michael edits narratives as needed before approving
  5. Time entry accuracy improves (real-time vs end-of-day memory)
  6. Reclaims 208 hours per year (50 min/day × 250 work days)

Outcome: Michael reclaims 208 hours per year. Even if he converts just 20% of that time to billable work, that's 42 hours × $600/hour = $25,200 in additional revenue. Plus reduced stress, improved accuracy, and better work-life balance. The productivity gain alone justifies the investment.

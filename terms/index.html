1. Privacy & Data Handling

Local Processing Architecture

LocalTranscribe processes audio transcription locally on your device during the transcription process. This local processing approach provides enhanced privacy control compared to cloud-based alternatives.

Important: Data Flow Accuracy

While transcription processing occurs locally, you control what data leaves your computer. The software includes optional integrations (such as Clio) and export features that allow you to share transcripts when you choose to do so. We do not automatically transmit your data, but you have the ability to export and share your transcripts through available integrations and export functions.

Data Collection & Website Analytics

  • Website Usage: We collect standard web analytics data (page views, user interactions, conversion events) to improve our services.
  • Account Information: We collect necessary account details (name, email, billing information) for service provision.
  • Support Data: Customer support interactions may be recorded for quality and training purposes.
  • No Audio Storage: We do not store your audio files or transcripts on our servers unless you explicitly use cloud-based features.

Third-Party Integrations

When you use optional integrations (such as Clio, practice management software, or cloud storage services), your data handling is subject to both our terms and the terms of those third-party services. You are responsible for understanding and agreeing to the data handling practices of any integrated services you choose to use.

Legal Transcription Challenges

Every law firm faces these critical issues with traditional transcription services

⚠️ Confidentiality Risk

Cloud-based transcription services expose sensitive attorney-client communications to data breaches, subpoenas, and unauthorized access by third parties.

💸 Unpredictable Costs

Per-minute pricing creates budget uncertainty. Long depositions, client meetings, and court proceedings lead to surprise bills that eat into firm profitability.

🔄 Workflow Disruption

Manual file uploads, download delays, and format incompatibilities disrupt legal workflows. Time spent managing transcription reduces billable hours.

📝 Quality Inconsistency

Generic transcription services struggle with legal terminology, case names, and procedural language, requiring extensive editing and review time.

Frequently Asked Questions

🚀 Getting Started

How do I get started with my free 5 minutes? +
Simply sign up for a free account, download the LocalTranscribe software, and you'll automatically receive 5 minutes of professional transcription each month. No credit card required for the free tier.
What audio formats does LocalTranscribe support? +
LocalTranscribe supports all major audio and video formats including MP3, WAV, M4A, MP4, MOV, AVI, and more. If you have an unusual format, our software will attempt to process it automatically.
How accurate is LocalTranscribe compared to human transcription? +
LocalTranscribe achieves 85-92% accuracy with our built-in editing tools to improve quality further. Combined with our punchlist workflow, you can achieve professional-grade results much faster than traditional transcription services.

💰 Billing & Pricing

How does volume pricing work? +
Our pricing automatically decreases per user as your team grows: $79/user (1-9 users), $69/user (10-24 users), $59/user (25+ users). Enterprise customers (50+ users) get custom pricing with additional discounts.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime? +
Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle. We handle proration automatically.
Is there a long-term contract required? +
No, all plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. You can cancel anytime. Enterprise customers may choose annual contracts for additional discounts.

🔧 Technical Support

What are the system requirements for LocalTranscribe? +
LocalTranscribe runs on Windows 10/11, macOS 10.14+, and Linux. Minimum requirements: 8GB RAM, 2GB free disk space, and a modern multi-core processor. More RAM and faster processors improve transcription speed.
Why is local processing better than cloud transcription? +
Local processing means your audio never leaves your computer, protecting attorney-client privilege and sensitive information. It's also faster (no upload time), works offline, and gives you complete control over your data.
What should I do if transcription seems inaccurate? +
Use our built-in editing tools and punchlist workflow to improve accuracy. Ensure good audio quality (clear speakers, minimal background noise) and use our confidence scoring to identify sections that may need review.

🔗 Integrations

How do I set up Clio integration? +
Connect your Clio account through the LocalTranscribe settings. Once connected, transcriptions can automatically create time entries and integrate with your billing workflow. We take 0% revenue share from this integration.
Are there other practice management integrations available? +
Currently, we offer deep integration with Clio. Additional practice management integrations are in development. Contact us if you have specific integration requirements for your practice.
Is there an API for custom integrations? +
API access is available for Enterprise customers. This allows custom integrations with your existing systems and workflows. Contact our sales team to discuss your specific API requirements.

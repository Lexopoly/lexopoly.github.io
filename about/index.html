About LocalTranscribe

Professional transcription software built for privacy, speed, and quality. Solving the legal industry's transcription crisis with local processing technology.

Our Mission

To provide professional-grade transcription software that respects your privacy, protects attorney-client privilege, and delivers the speed and accuracy modern legal practices demand—all while keeping your data completely under your control.

Our Story

LocalTranscribe emerged from a simple realization: legal professionals need transcription technology that prioritizes privacy as much as accuracy.

While cloud-based services expose sensitive conversations to data breaches and compromise attorney-client privilege, we took a different approach—building professional transcription software that processes everything locally on your computer.

The result is software that's 28x faster than real-time, maintains 85-92% accuracy with editing tools, and ensures your conversations never leave your control.

Why Local Processing Matters

  • Attorney-Client Privilege: No cloud exposure means complete confidentiality protection
  • HIPAA Compliance: Supports healthcare privacy requirements by design
  • Speed: No upload delays—process immediately on your hardware
  • Control: You decide what data goes where and when
  • Reliability: No internet outages or service disruptions
  • Cost Predictability: No per-minute fees or surprise charges

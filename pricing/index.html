Frequently Asked Questions

How do volume discounts work?
Our pricing automatically decreases per user as your team grows: $79/user (1-9 users), $69/user (10-24 users), $59/user (25+ users). Enterprise customers (50+ users) get custom pricing with additional discounts.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle. We'll handle the proration automatically.
Is there a long-term contract required?
No, all plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. You can cancel anytime. Enterprise customers may choose annual contracts for additional discounts.
How does the demo process work?
Request a demo to see LocalTranscribe in action with your own audio files. We'll show you the 28x speed processing, editing workflow, and Clio integration tailored to your specific use case.

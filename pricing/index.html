Frequently Asked Questions

Why is LocalTranscribe more expensive than per-minute services?
LocalTranscribe offers unlimited transcription with local processing privacy and professional editing tools. At just 5.3 hours of Rev.com usage per month, Professional tier pays for itself while providing unlimited usage and zero cloud exposure.
How do volume discounts work?
Our pricing automatically decreases per user as your team grows: $79/user (1-9 users), $69/user (10-24 users), $59/user (25+ users). Enterprise customers (50+ users) get custom pricing with additional discounts.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle. We'll handle the proration automatically.
Is there a long-term contract required?
No, all plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. You can cancel anytime. Enterprise customers may choose annual contracts for additional discounts.
What's included in the 5 free minutes?
Your 5 free minutes include full LocalTranscribe quality (85-92% accuracy), local processing, and basic export formats. It's a complete demonstration of our professional features.
Does Clio integration cost extra?
No! Unlike other providers, our Clio integration has zero revenue sharing or additional fees. You keep 100% of your revenue while getting seamless time entry and billing integration.

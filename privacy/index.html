1. Overview

LocalTranscribe, operated by Lexopoly LLC ("we," "us," or "our"), is committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the confidentiality of your data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our professional transcription software and services.

Local Processing

Audio transcription happens entirely on your computer - never on our servers.

Minimal Data Collection

We only collect essential account and billing information.

GDPR Compliant

Full compliance with EU data protection regulations.

Your Control

You decide what data to share and can delete your account anytime.

2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create a LocalTranscribe account, we collect:

  • Email address (required for account access and communication)
  • Name (for billing and customer service)
  • Company information (optional, for business accounts)
  • Password (encrypted and stored securely)

2.2 Billing Information

For paid subscriptions, we collect:

  • Billing address
  • Payment method information (processed by our secure payment providers)
  • Transaction history and invoices

2.3 Usage Analytics

We collect minimal, anonymized usage data to improve our service:

  • Number of transcriptions processed (not the content)
  • Software version and performance metrics
  • Feature usage patterns (which features are used, not what data is processed)
  • Error logs and crash reports (containing no personal audio data)

2.4 What We DO NOT Collect

ðŸš« Zero Audio Data Collection

We never collect, store, or process your audio files, transcription content, or any sensitive data from your transcriptions. All processing happens locally on your device.

3. Local Processing Commitment

LocalTranscribe's core privacy principle: Your audio and transcription data never leaves your computer unless you explicitly choose to export or share it.

3.1 How Local Processing Works

  • Audio files are processed entirely on your local machine
  • Transcription happens using local AI models
  • No audio data is transmitted to our servers or any cloud service
  • You control all export and sharing of transcription results

3.2 Benefits for Legal and Healthcare Professionals

  • Attorney-Client Privilege: Protected by design - no cloud exposure risk
  • HIPAA Compliance Support: Local processing supports healthcare privacy requirements
  • Confidential Sources: Journalists can protect source identity completely
  • Government Security: Meets strict data handling requirements

4. How We Use Your Data

We use the limited data we collect for these specific purposes:

4.1 Account Management

  • Providing access to your LocalTranscribe account
  • Authenticating your identity
  • Managing your subscription and billing
  • Providing customer support

4.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve software performance
  • Identifying and fixing bugs
  • Developing new features based on user needs
  • Ensuring software security and stability

4.3 Communication

  • Sending important service updates and security notifications
  • Providing technical support
  • Responding to your inquiries
  • Sending billing and payment notifications

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for commercial purposes.

5.2 Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in these specific circumstances:

Service Providers

  • Payment processors (Stripe) for billing
  • Email service providers for account communications
  • Analytics services (with anonymized data only)

Legal Requirements

  • When required by law or valid legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users
  • To prevent fraud or security threats

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity, subject to the same privacy protections.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

6.1 Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) for all data transmission
  • Encryption at rest for stored account data
  • Secure password hashing using industry best practices
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Multi-factor authentication options

6.2 Operational Safeguards

  • Limited employee access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular security training for staff
  • Incident response procedures
  • Regular backups with encryption

6.3 Your Local Security

Since transcription data stays on your computer, we recommend:

  • Using strong passwords for your device
  • Keeping your operating system updated
  • Using antivirus software
  • Securing your local transcription files appropriately

7. Your Rights and Controls

You have significant control over your data and privacy:

7.1 Access and Portability

  • View and download your account information
  • Export your account data in a portable format
  • Request copies of data we have about you

7.2 Correction and Updates

  • Update your account information at any time
  • Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Modify your communication preferences

7.3 Deletion and Account Management

  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Request deletion of specific information
  • Cancel your subscription at any time

7.4 GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you're in the European Union, you also have the right to:

  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Restrict processing under certain circumstances
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@lexopoly.com or use the data controls in your account settings. We'll respond within 30 days.

8. Third-Party Services

8.1 Payment Processing

We use Stripe for secure payment processing. Stripe's privacy policy governs how they handle your payment information.

8.2 Analytics

We may use analytics services to understand how our software is used. These services receive only anonymized, aggregated data with no personally identifiable information.

8.3 Integration Partners

When you use integrations (like Clio), data sharing is controlled by you and governed by the integration partner's privacy policy. We facilitate the connection but don't store integration data.

8.4 No Advertising Networks

We do not use advertising networks or tracking pixels for marketing purposes.

9. International Users

9.1 Data Transfers

LocalTranscribe is operated from the United States. If you're located outside the US, your account information may be transferred to and processed in the United States.

9.2 EU-US Data Protection

For European users, we provide adequate protection for your data through:

  • GDPR compliance measures
  • Standard contractual clauses with service providers
  • Minimized data collection and local processing
  • Strong security safeguards

9.3 Local Processing Advantage

Since your transcription data never leaves your computer, international data transfer concerns are minimized to just account and billing information.

10. Children's Privacy

LocalTranscribe is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at privacy@lexopoly.com.

11. Changes to This Policy

  • We'll notify you of significant changes via email
  • We'll post a notice in the LocalTranscribe software
  • Changes take effect 30 days after notification

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
